The Future Of Art Starts
At Yale

The School of Art is one of Yale's Graduate & Professional Schools conferring MFA degrees in Graphic Design, Painting/Printmaking, Photography, and Sculpture; and offers undergraduate-level art courses to Yale College students.

Why Yale?

World-renowned faculty

Learn from influential artists, scholars, and critics whose work shapes the global art world.

Cutting-edge facilities

Create, experiment, and refine your craft in state-of-the-art studios, labs, and workshops.

Interdisciplinary opportunities

Collaborate across Yale departments to broaden your artistic perspective.
